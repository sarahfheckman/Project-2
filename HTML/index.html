Does Gross Domestic Product affect water availability

Water is Life and is fundermental to human health and survival. Securing access to safe drinking water for all would go a long way to reducing illness and death.

In this study, we will be looking at the correlation between Gross Domestic Product (GDP) and Water. We are seeking to find if contries with a high GDP have better drinking water than countries with a lower GDP .
