Total improved Water Accesibility compared to GDP

The data is index by 100, the closer a country is to a hundred, the better drinking water they have.According to the plot, there is no correlation between GDP and better drinking water availability . A country with a higher GDP doesnt imply that they have better of good quality drinking water.

For Example, the USA has a GDP of about 18 trillion, but there are several parts of the US that do have good quality water like Michigan.
